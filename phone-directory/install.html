Install the app for quick access

Follow the instructions below to add this directory to your home screen

In Safari
1
Tap the ⋯ button at the bottom right of the toolbar
2
Tap "Share" from the menu
3
Tap "View More" at the bottom of the share sheet
4
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
5
Tap "Add" in the top right
In Chrome
1
Tap the Share button in the address bar
2
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
3
Tap "Add" to confirm
1
Tap the ⋮ menu in the top right of Chrome
2
Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
3
Tap "Install" to confirm
or follow the steps above

Once installed, the app works offline too
